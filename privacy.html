Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 2, 2025

Thank you for visiting Lunch Cupid. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website (lunchcupid.com) and use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect two types of information from you:

  • Email Address: When you voluntarily sign up for our daily email service.
  • Zip Code: When you voluntarily provide it in our signup form.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Send Daily Emails: Your email address is used to send you the daily lunch recommendation as described.
  • To Personalize Content: Your zip code is used to determine your time zone (for email delivery timing) and, in the future, may be used to provide more relevant local recommendations.

3. Information Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. Your information is managed through the following third-party services that are essential to our operation:

  • Email Marketing Service (e.g., MailerLite): Your email address and zip code are stored with our email service provider for the sole purpose of managing our subscriber list and sending our daily emails.
  • Website Hosting (GitHub Pages): Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. They may collect standard server logs (like IP addresses) for security and operational purposes.

4. Your Rights and Choices (Unsubscribing)

You have the right to opt-out of our email list at any time. Every email we send includes a clear "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom. Once you unsubscribe, you will be permanently removed from our mailing list.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. We recommend you review this page periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@lunchcupid.com
